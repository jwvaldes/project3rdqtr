    • As most of us may have already known, this month of March is Fire-Prevention Month! In honor of celebrating Fire-Prevention Month at these trying times of the pandemic I have decided to put two and two together and make a small blog on...

    Lessen the amount of plugged objects! When you aren't using something, it is best that you unplug it until the next time you need it! Gadgets that have been plugged for too long may overheat which can cause fires!
    Keep flammable objects away from anything that could start a fire! This is already kind of self-explanatory. Keep flammable objects away from anything that can start a fire in order to not let the fire spread or ignite!
    If you sense something burning, check it out immediately! It wouldn't hurt to be careful sometimes, especilly if it could save you and your family's lives! Once you see or smell smoke, please seek its source and get ready to put it out before it gets bigger and gets its flames to consume more of your home if it really is a fire!

    That's all I have for today! Thanks for listening!

    And remember, kids! Don't touch blue fire! It is not cold, no matter how much your older sibling says it is!
